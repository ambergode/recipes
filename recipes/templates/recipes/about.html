SeeFood

SeeFood is here to help inspire good choices and planning. Upload scrumptious looking photos of your favorite dishes along with their accompanying recipes, and then build meal plans and shopping lists all in one place.

This project springs from my husband's and my daily need to eat something and the uninspired choices we make in that process. There are lots of delicious, healthy foods that we both love but forget about - "Out of sight, out of mind," and all that - and usually reached for what we could see. We dreamt of a way to bring more organization and inspiration to our meals, and from that desire was born SeeFood!

- Amber Oakley, creator
